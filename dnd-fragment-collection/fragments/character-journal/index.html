Backstory

Born in the small village of Millhaven, Kaelen grew up as the son of a blacksmith. His life changed forever when an ancient tome was discovered in the family's basement, revealing his lineage to a powerful wizard who once protected the realm. Now, he travels the land seeking to understand his magical heritage while protecting innocent people from the rising darkness that threatens the kingdom.
Birthplace: Millhaven Village
Family: Marcus (Father, Blacksmith), Elena (Mother, Herbalist)
Defining Event: Discovery of Ancestral Spellbook

Session Notes

Session #12 March 15, 2024 The Whispering Woods
Important Combat
Discovered the ancient ruins mentioned in grandfather's journal. Encountered shadow creatures guarding a mysterious crystal. After a fierce battle, we retrieved the crystal and learned it contains trapped memories of the past. This could be the key to understanding what happened to my ancestor.
Key NPCs: Shadow Guardian, Elara the Sage
Loot Acquired: Memory Crystal, 150 gold, Scroll of Protection
XP Gained: 1,200 XP
Session #11 March 8, 2024 Redbrook Village
Social Investigation
Arrived in Redbrook to investigate bandit attacks. Spoke with villagers and learned the bandits have been organized and well-equipped. Found evidence suggesting they're being supplied by someone with significant resources. The village elder mentioned strange lights in the nearby forest.
Session #10 March 1, 2024 Millhaven Village
Family Downtime
Returned home to Millhaven for a brief rest. Helped father in the smithy and researched family history with mother. Found a hidden compartment in the basement containing more of grandfather's notes. The notes mention a "Guardian's Trial" in the Whispering Woods.
